How the estimate is framed

The displayed range is based on static public-price assumptions for copper, connector tiers, processing points, and application-specific trade multipliers.

Application Multipliers
  • Industrial Automation: 1.3x - 1.6x
  • RV & Marine: 1.4x - 1.8x
  • Data Center: 1.5x - 1.9x
  • Medical Device: 1.7x - 2.2x
  • EV High-Voltage: 1.8x - 2.3x
What is included

Material logic, connector tier benchmarks, basic processing points, special-process uplifts, and low-volume tooling/NRE allowance where applicable.

What is not included

Freight, taxes, unusual customer-owned tooling, certification audits, and project-specific validation plans beyond typical market assumptions.
